# Company Scoring Data Sources

Company and brand context should supplement product-level evidence, not replace it.

Candidate sources:
- Brand sustainability pages
- Parent company sustainability reports
- Certification registry records
- Regulatory and enforcement records
- Marketplace product listings
- Open product datasets where licensing permits use
- Admin-entered research notes with provenance

Rules:
- Every source record needs source URL, source date, retrieval date, access method, license status, and evidence tier.
- AI may extract, match, summarize, and explain sourced records.
- AI must not create unsupported sustainability facts.
- Paid brand relationships must never improve a rating.
