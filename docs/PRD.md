# SusCo PRD

SusCo is a premium sustainability-scored shopping intelligence platform with two product verticals:

1. Sustainability-scored product search
2. Personal sustainability tracker / show-off profile

Core product line: Search. Score. Compare. Choose. Track.

The consumer flow starts with natural product search across commerce platforms, then exposes sustainability rating, confidence, evidence, missing data, better alternatives, and original-platform click-out links. The retention flow starts when a user saves, buys, avoids, or compares a product, then updates tracker stats, badges, and shareable profile cards.

SusCo is not an NGO dashboard, government ESG portal, generic affiliate marketplace, or paid brand ranking system. Brands cannot buy better ratings. AI can summarize evidence, but must not invent sustainability facts.
